Portable Unitary Throwable Target Interceptor (P.U.T.T.I)

The P.U.T.T.I is a portable device that detects objects within a range of 5 feet or greater in front of the device developed as part of the ECE department at the University of Central Florida as part of Senior Design. A laser and buzzer will be activated to provide a visual and audible notification for users of the device. The device utilizes both YOLO detection through the camera as well as measurements achieved through the radar modules to accurately detect the objects, ensuring that the device does not target people or other objects.
